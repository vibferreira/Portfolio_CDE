About me

Hello, my name is Vitoria Barbosa Ferreira, I am a Graduate Student from Brazil, sponsored by the Erasmus + EMJMD programme. I am very passionate about getting in touch with multicultural environments and interested in everything that has a spatial dimension.

Shortly about my background, I have a major in Geography and a minor in Teaching Geography by the University of Sao Paulo (Sao Paulo, Brazil). Currently, I am pursuing the Copernicus Master in Digital Earth (CDE). The opportunity to attend the CDE programme is helping me to boost my understanding of map elaboration and spatial analysis since it is particularly focused in geoinformatics. Within this master I am developing skills in spatial analysis, research communication, remote sensing and programming applied to the Geo field.
